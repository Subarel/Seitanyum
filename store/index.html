What is Seitan?

Seitan (say-TAN) is a plant-based protein from wheat gluten — eaten in Asia for over a thousand years, now loved worldwide.

Often called "vegan chicken" because of its satisfying texture. It absorbs marinades beautifully — that's why our menu tastes genuinely great.

Completely soy-free — perfect for anyone with soy sensitivities.
